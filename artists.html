J Cole

J. Cole is also a hip hop/rap artist. He is similar to Kendrick Lamar by their skills in creating bars. While J.Cole speaks more about society and justice; Kendrick Lamar raps more about life and religion. I'd say that the two of them together in a track are perfect. One of my favorites by J.Cole is "Let Nas Down" from his Born Sinner album.

Joey Bada$$

Joey is very similar to Kendrick Lamar in my opinion. They both rap about a lot of the same content. You can even compare Joey to Ab-Soul and Earl Sweatshirt. Joey is one of those rappers who are very lyrical if they have a good beat to the track. One of my favorites by Joey is "Killuminati" from his album 1999.
